【■ SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE: TRI-LAYER VERTICAL INTEGRATION】
The technical superiority of the LEVI computational system lies in the architecture of its "Cognition Loop."

■ 1. Observation (Data Acquisition Layer): 
Monitoring all transactions on the Tempo Network at millisecond speeds. It captures liquidity migrations, wallet cluster shifts, and real-time bytecode alterations in smart contracts. This layer functions as the system's "Sensory Nervous System," capturing the genesis of all information pulse points.

■ 2. Intelligence (Reasoning Layer): 
Raw unstructured data is processed through independent reasoning cores optimized for three depths of perception (Core / Analytics / Deep). This is not merely data organization; it is the derivation of the "Logical Motive" behind the data.

■ 3. Execution (Output Layer): 
In the final stage, reasoning results are converted into structured intelligence reports or autonomous triggers. This includes not only signal transmission but also future-facing autonomous trade execution protocols.
