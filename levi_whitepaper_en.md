1. OBJECTIVE & SYSTEM OVERVIEW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

LEVI is an **"Autonomous Cognition Infrastructure"** (ACI) engineered for advanced data processing and multi-agent reasoning within the Tempo Network ecosystem.

The primary obstacle in current on-chain research is not the absence of data, but a massive "Reasoning Deficit." Standard analytics fail to extract motive from the noise of decentralized transactions. LEVI vertically integrates high-frequency data observation, hierarchical reasoning cores, and autonomous execution triggers into a single unified infrastructure layer. It exists to form systemic order from the sea of unstructured data.

2. SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE: THE COGNITION LOOP
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The LEVI processing core is built on a proprietary "Cognition Loop" consisting of three distinct functional layers:

■ LAYER 1: OBSERVATION (Data Acquisition)
As the system's sensory input, this layer monitors all Tempo heartbeat pulses at millisecond speeds. It captures transactional clusters, liquidity migrations, and bytecode-level alterations in smart contracts. This allows the system to detect precursors to market movements before they are indexed by traditional dashboards.

■ LAYER 2: INTELLIGENCE (Hierarchical Reasoning)
Unstructured data is processed through independent reasoning cores. Hierarchical reasoning is assigned based on the required compute depth of the query:
- LEVI CORE: Designed for high-speed data integrity validation and baseline metric extraction.
- ANALYTICAL ENGINE: Focused on multidimensional behavioral cluster analysis and whale correlation mapping.
- DEEP COGNITION: Flagship multi-agent coordination for deep protocol auditing and predictive modeling.

■ LAYER 3: EXECUTION (Output Integration)
Finalizing reasoning results into structured institutional reports or autonomous triggers (e.g., risk signaling, automated trade execution, or protocol-level hedging).

4. ECONOMIC SUSTAINABILITY: THE DEFLATIONARY ENGINE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

■ COMPUTE FUEL BURN (FEE BURN)
LEVI implements a "Utility-Driven Deflationary Economy." For every reasoning task executed on the platform, users pay $LEVI as "Compute Fuel." A fixed percentage of tokens spent on these tasks is permanently burned from the circulating supply. This forces token scarcity as a direct consequence of platform utility.

■ 30-DAY ACCESS COMMITMENT
To ensure system stability, platform access is gated by a 30-day "Commitment Term." Tokens are non-withdrawable during this initial month of utilization. While withdrawal is possible after 30 days, the moment a user unstakes, all access to the intelligence stack is immediately revoked. This acts as a specialization filter for dedicated participants.
